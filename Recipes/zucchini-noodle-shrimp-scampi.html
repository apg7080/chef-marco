Zucchini noodle shrimp scampi

A fast skillet dinner that splits clean: two adult plates deglazed with white wine and finished with lemon, parsley, and a little heat, plus a quiet baby bowl of short-snipped zoodles and minced shrimp set aside before the salt and wine. Twenty-five minutes from cold pan to plated, no prep dependency.

03

Method

25 minutes of active time.

  1. 2 min Bloom the garlic.

    Melt butter with the olive oil in a wide skillet over medium. Add minced garlic and stir until fragrant and barely golden, about a minute.

  2. 3 min Cook the shrimp.

    Add shrimp in a single layer. Cook until pink and opaque, about three minutes, turning once. Pull a few to the side and chop fine for the baby portion.

  3. 2 min Wilt the zoodles.

    Add the spiralized zucchini and toss in the garlic butter just until softened, about two minutes. Do not overcook or they weep.

  4. 1 min Set baby portion aside.

    Spoon a small mound of zoodles into a shallow bowl. Snip the strands into short pieces with kitchen shears and fold in the finely minced shrimp and a drizzle of olive oil. No salt, no wine, no zest yet.

  5. 2 min Finish the adult plates.

    Pour the white wine into the pan and let it bubble down for a minute. Stir in lemon juice, lemon zest, parsley, flaky salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Plate at once.

04

Plate it

One safe choice in each grip category.

  • Spoon load. Pre-load a baby spoon with short zoodles and minced shrimp.
  • Pinch. Loose shrimp mince and short noodle pieces for thumb-and-finger practice.
  • Strip grip. Steamed zucchini spear on the side for a confident grip.

Zoodles release water as they sit. Drain the baby bowl before serving.

Safety check

From the Chef Marco Baby Safety Notes for a 12-month-old.

Pass

  • No salt added at any step
  • No honey
  • No choke shapes: whole grape, whole blueberry, round meatball, whole nut, hard raw carrot, hard raw apple, whole cherry tomato
  • Texture is soft pinch
  • Only 4 teeth (front incisors), no molars yet: every piece must squish between finger and thumb, nothing that needs grinding
  • Contains common allergens: shellfish, milk. Confirm none are on the no-go list for this child.
  • Sit-down eating only, never propped or in a moving stroller
06

Storage and reheat

Glass jars only. No plastic for warm food.

  • Fridge. 2 days in sealed glass.
  • Freezer. Up to 0 weeks. Thaw overnight in the fridge.
  • Reheat. Gentle skillet over low or microwave fifteen seconds, stir, fifteen more. Wrist test.
  • Do not. Freeze. Zucchini turns to water on thaw. Reheat the baby portion more than once.
07

Adult version

Same base, one extra minute, fully seasoned.

After the baby portion is sealed, deglaze the pan with dry white wine, then finish with lemon juice, lemon zest, chopped parsley, flaky salt, cracked pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Plate over the zoodles and shrimp with a lemon wedge. Add a hunk of crusty bread to mop the garlic butter or a simple arugula salad if you want a third element.
